Ordering from Publications Plus is straightforward whether you're a first-time customer or a returning client. You don't need to create an account — our online store at [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net) supports guest checkout so you can go from product selection to confirmed order in just a few minutes. All payments are handled securely through Stripe, and once your order is placed, our press team takes it from there. The sections below walk you through every step of the process.

## The Order Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse & Configure Your Product">
    Head to [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net) and find the product that matches your project — business cards, brochures, magazines, banners, direct mail, and more. Select your desired **size**, **paper stock**, **quantity**, and any finishing options (lamination, UV coating, binding, etc.). Prices recalculate in real time as you make each selection, so you always see the exact cost before adding to your cart.

    <Tip>
      Not sure which product fits your project? Call us at [(877) 701-5087](tel:+18777015087) or email [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) and we'll point you in the right direction.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Out as a Guest">
    Add your configured product to the cart and proceed to checkout. **No account is required** — simply enter your contact information and shipping or pickup preference, then complete payment through our secure Stripe integration. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately after checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Your Artwork">
    After your order is confirmed, a member of our team will reach out to you by email to collect your print-ready files. Attach your files to that email reply, or follow the upload instructions provided. If you don't yet have print-ready artwork, our in-house **Design Department** can create it for you — just mention that when you respond.

    <Note>
      Make sure your files meet our specs before sending: PDF format, 300 DPI, CMYK color mode, and 0.25″ bleed on all sides. See [File Requirements](/file-requirements) for the full checklist.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-Press Review">
    Once your artwork is received, our Pre-Press team reviews every file for resolution, color accuracy, bleed, and font integrity before the job goes to press. If there's an issue that could affect print quality, we'll contact you to resolve it — we never run a job we're not confident in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Production">
    Your job moves to the appropriate press department — Digital, Sheet-Fed, Heat-Set, Wide-Format, or another — based on your product type and quantity. Our press operators manage the entire production run to ensure consistent quality from the first sheet to the last.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Shipping or Local Pickup">
    When production is complete, your order ships via your chosen carrier or is held for local pickup at our facility at 884 Lagoon Commercial Blvd, Montgomery, AL 36117. You'll receive a carrier email notification when your order is on its way or ready for collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Production Timelines

Production time begins after your artwork has been received, approved, and cleared by Pre-Press. The timeline depends on the press method required for your product.

| Press Method | Typical Production Time |
| --- | --- |
| **Digital Printing** | 2–3 business days |
| **Offset (Sheet-Fed / Heat-Set)** | 5–7 business days |
| **Wide-Format** | 5–7 business days |
| **Simple / Small Jobs** | As little as under 1 hour |
| **Complex or Large-Volume Jobs** | Several business days |

<Warning>
  Production timelines do not include shipping transit time. If you have a hard deadline, contact us at [production@pubplus.net](mailto:production@pubplus.net) **before** placing your order so we can confirm availability and advise on rush options.
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Do I need an account to place an order?">
  No. Our online store at [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net) supports full guest checkout. You only need to provide your contact details and payment information at checkout — no registration required. If you're an existing client looking to manage your projects, you can log in to the client portal at [client.pubplus.net](https://client.pubplus.net).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What file formats do you accept?">
  We prefer **PDF** for all print-ready artwork because it reliably preserves fonts, colors, and bleed settings across platforms. We also accept native **Adobe Illustrator (.ai)** and **Adobe InDesign (.indd)** files. All files must be at least 300 DPI, set to CMYK color mode, and include 0.25″ bleed on all four sides. Fonts must be embedded or converted to outlines. See our full [File Requirements](/file-requirements) page for details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How long does production take?">
  Digital print jobs typically complete in **2–3 business days** after artwork approval. Offset and wide-format jobs take **5–7 business days**. Simple, small-quantity jobs can sometimes be turned around in under an hour. Production time does not include shipping. If you have a deadline, reach out to [production@pubplus.net](mailto:production@pubplus.net) before ordering.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a custom quote for a large or unique project?">
  Yes. If your project involves custom sizes, specialty materials, very high quantities, or services not listed in the online store, contact us for a quote. Email [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) or call [(877) 701-5087](tel:+18777015087) with your project details, and we'll provide a tailored estimate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the refund and reprint policy?">
  Because print products are produced to your specific configuration and artwork, we handle quality concerns on a case-by-case basis. If your order arrives with a production defect — a press error, color inconsistency on our end, or a manufacturing flaw — contact us at [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) with photos of the issue and your order number. We'll work with you to make it right. Please note that errors resulting from customer-supplied artwork that was not caught during the Pre-Press review (such as low resolution or incorrect color mode) may not qualify for a reprint at no charge, which is why we strongly encourage reviewing the [File Requirements](/file-requirements) before submitting.
</Accordion>
