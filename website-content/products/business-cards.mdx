Whether you're networking at an event, leaving cards at the front desk, or stocking a trade-show table, a well-printed business card makes a lasting first impression. Publications Plus produces short-run business cards in-house through our digital department, with a turnaround of **2–3 business days**. Choose from three finish types — Coated, Gloss, or Smooth (Soft Touch) — each with its own feel, look, and coating options. Prices start at $0.30 per card** with a **$**75 minimum order**.

<Note>
  **File preparation requirements:** Submit print-ready files as **PDF** at **300 DPI**, in **CMYK color mode**, with a **0.25" bleed** on all sides. Files that do not meet these specs may delay your order or result in color/quality differences from what you expect on screen.
</Note>

## Choose Your Card Type

Use the tabs below to explore each finish type and its available options. Not sure which to pick? See the [comparison guide](#choosing-the-right-finish) after the tabs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Coated">
    Coated business cards are printed on thick cardstock with a protective coating that can be applied to one or both sides. The standout feature of this type is the **single-side coating option** — coat only the front for a polished presentation while leaving the back uncoated so recipients can jot down notes or personalize the card.

    **Pricing:** Starting at $0.30/card — $75 minimum

    | Option | Choices |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Corner Style** | Rounded Corners, Square Corners |
    | **Additional Coating** | UV Coating, None |
    | **Thickness** | 16pt Cardstock, 14pt Cardstock |
    | **Sides Coated** | Coated Both Sides, Coated Single Side |
    | **Printed Sides** | Printed Both Sides, Printed Single Side |
    | **Print Color** | Black & White, Full Color |
    | **Quantity** | 250–10,000 (in increments of 250) |

    <Tip>
      Choose **Coated Single Side** if you want a glossy front and a writable back — ideal for appointment cards, referral cards, or any design where the recipient needs to fill in information.
    </Tip>

    <Tip>
      Add **UV Coating** for cards that will be handled frequently or exposed to moisture. The extra layer significantly increases durability and gives the surface a crisp, high-gloss sheen.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Gloss">
    Gloss business cards deliver a bright, high-shine finish that makes colors pop and photography look vibrant. If your brand relies on bold imagery or saturated color, gloss stock is a strong choice. This type uses a different stock designation system (pound weights) rather than point thicknesses.

    **Pricing:** Starting at $0.30/card — $75 minimum

    | Option | Choices |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Additional Coating** | UV Coating, No Coating |
    | **Corner Style** | Rounded Corners, Square Corners |
    | **Thickness** | #130, #100 |
    | **Print Color** | Black & White, Full Color |
    | **Printed Sides** | Both Sides, Single Side |
    | **Quantity** | 250–10,000 (in increments of 250) |

    <Tip>
      The **#130 stock** is heavier and feels more substantial in hand — a great choice when you want your card to convey premium quality right from the first touch.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Smooth (Soft Touch)">
    Smooth (Soft Touch) cards have a matte, velvety finish that feels distinctly different from coated or gloss stock. The tactile quality is a conversation starter on its own, and the subdued surface lets typography-forward or minimalist designs breathe. Note that the soft-touch coating is applied uniformly — spot coating and writable backs are not available on this stock.

    **Pricing:** Starting at $0.30/card — $75 minimum

    | Option | Choices |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Corner Style** | Rounded Corners, Square Corners |
    | **Thickness** | #100, #130 |
    | **Printed Sides** | Both Sides, Single Side |
    | **Print Color** | Black & White, Full Color |
    | **Quantity** | 250–10,000 (in increments of 250) |

    <Tip>
      Soft Touch cards can show fingerprints more readily than gloss stock. If your design includes large dark or solid-color areas, be aware of this and handle finished cards carefully during distribution.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Choosing the Right Finish

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Coated" icon="droplet">
    Best when you need flexibility — opt for single-side coating to keep one face writable. Offers the widest range of coating and thickness combinations, including optional UV protection.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Gloss" icon="star">
    Best for image-heavy or color-rich designs that benefit from a reflective, high-shine surface. Similar in appearance to coated cards but uses a gloss-specific stock for extra luminance.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Smooth (Soft Touch)" icon="hand">
    Best for brands that want a premium, understated tactile experience. The matte velvety feel stands out in any stack of cards and complements clean, modern design aesthetics.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Production & Finishing

Our digital department handles all short-run business card orders. Standard production time is **2–3 business days** after your print-ready file is approved. Finishing options — including **cutting** and **round corners** — are completed in-house.

<Note>
  Rounded corners are a finishing operation performed after printing. If you select rounded corners, make sure your design does not place critical content (logos, text, QR codes) within 0.125" of any edge to avoid clipping during the corner-rounding process.
</Note>
