#### FAQ

Do I need an account to order?

No account needed. Browse products, add to cart, and check out as a guest. You'll receive order confirmation and updates by email.

What file formats do you accept?

Print-ready PDFs are preferred. We also accept AI and InDesign files. Files should be 300 DPI with proper bleed and crop marks. Not sure? Email us at [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) before ordering.

How long does production take?

Turnaround varies by product. Digital prints are typically 2–3 business days; offset and wide-format runs may take 5–7 days depending on quantity. You'll see estimated timelines at checkout.

Can I get a custom quote for something not in the store?

Absolutely. The store covers our most common products, but we print a lot more than what's listed. [Submit a quote request](https://pubplus.net/quote-request/) and our team will get back to you quickly.

What's your refund policy?

Because print products are custom-produced, we do not accept returns on correctly printed orders. If there's a production error on our end, we'll reprint or refund — no questions asked. See our full [Refund Policy](https://store.pubplus.net/refund-policy).
