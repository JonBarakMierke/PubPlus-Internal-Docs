# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Publications Plus, Inc. collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1\. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide such as name, email, billing details, shipping address, and order history. We also collect technical data such as IP address and browser information.

## 2\. How We Use Information

We use your information to process orders, provide customer support, improve our services, and prevent fraud.

## 3\. Third-Party Services

We use third-party providers such as Stripe for payment processing and may use analytics tools to improve performance. These services have their own privacy policies.

## 4\. QR Code & Tracking Data

Some products may include QR codes that track scan activity (such as time, device type, and approximate location) for reporting and analytics purposes.

## 5\. Data Retention

We retain order and customer data as long as necessary for business and legal obligations.

## 6\. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at support@pubplus.net.
