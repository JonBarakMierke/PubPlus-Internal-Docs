# Refund Policy

Because most of our products are custom printed, our refund policy is limited and depends on the nature of the issue.

## 1\. Custom Orders

Custom printed products cannot be refunded once production has begun, unless there is a verified defect or production error.

## 2\. Proof Approval

Customers are responsible for approving all artwork and proofs. Approved designs are considered final.

## 3\. Errors & Defects

If a product arrives damaged or incorrect due to our error, we will reprint or replace the order at no cost.

## 4\. Chargebacks

We encourage customers to contact us directly before initiating a chargeback so we can resolve issues quickly.

## 5\. Non-Refundable Items

Shipping costs and completed custom production work are non-refundable unless otherwise required by law.
