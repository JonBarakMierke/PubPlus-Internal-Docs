# Custom Shape Outdoor Sign

Martin Collision Center

![Man in bucket truck looking at building with a green and orange sign on it](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/270/01KTACYKHFYATRE6V7GXNMS4Q6.webp)

### Refreshing an old sign

Sometimes, the best sign is the one you already had. At Martin Collision Center, our recent project didn’t involve a flashy redesign or a new concept; it was all about breathing new life into a tried-and-true display. The original signs had served their purpose well, but were fading due to not having been laminated. Now, it was simply time for a refresh and an added layer of durability thanks to some UV laminate. 
 
There’s something deeply satisfying about this kind of job. The client knew it worked, and we knew it would work again. It’s a testament to the power of quality design and materials that stand the test of time.

### Fast, Hassle-Free Installation

At Martin Collision Center, our team demonstrated how smooth and convenient the sign replacement process can be when experience meets preparation. 
 
From the moment artwork was approved, our production team got to work, delivering a finished product within days. Our installation crew arrived on-site and had both signs installed in only a few hours. No delays, no disruption, just fast, efficient service that lets the Martin Collision team get right back to doing what they do best.

![Man in bucket truck installing a green and orange outdoor sign on wooden background](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/271/01KTAD05M984H9G9TR5GE4VYJM.webp)

![Long rectangular outdoor sign on the side of a building](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/272/01KTAD1C100TGD1XY73800XVTC.webp)

### The versatility of Max Metal®

Max Metal® is a go-to material for good reason. It’s lightweight yet incredibly strong, making it ideal for outdoor signage that needs to endure tough conditions without sagging, warping, or fading. And when paired with a UV laminate coating it can withstand even the worst of the Alabama sun. 
 
This project is a perfect showcase of what Max Metal® can do. One of the signs was a clean, large-format rectangle. The other was a custom-cut shape that matched the client’s branding, as it was cut to the shape of their logo. Whether you need sharp edges, sleek curves, or simple durability, Max Metal® is likely the material for the job.
