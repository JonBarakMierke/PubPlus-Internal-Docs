# Window Decal

Cork & Cleaver

![White decals on glass door](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/282/01KTAD8YG4A5PM4S13E9D31HQX.webp)

### Simple & Clean

When it comes to window decals, less is often more. In our experience, white vinyl is the best choice for visibility and clarity. 
 
While colorful and intricate designs can be tempting, they can easily get lost in glare, reflections, or inconsistent lighting. White, on the other hand, consistently stands out, especially against dark glass. Why? Because exterior sunlight is typically much brighter than the lighting inside, it creates the perfect contrast that allows a clean white decal to shine. It’s an elegant solution that works across all times of day and seasons, and it looks sharp doing it.

### Decals that Last

One of the underrated strengths of white vinyl decals is their durability. Unlike printed graphics (that haven’t been laminated) that can fade with time and exposure, these decals are made of solid white vinyl, no inks, no worries. 
This means they not only look crisp on day one, but will continue to hold their shape, color, and clean aesthetic for years to come. It’s a low-maintenance, long-term solution that’s ideal for any business wanting a professional and permanent feel without constant upkeep.

### Form Follows Function

In this case, like most door decals, the decal wasn’t meant to be a centerpiece; it was meant to serve a purpose. A simple white vinyl application lets the restaurant display its name, website address, and hours clearly to guests and passersby without introducing extra design noise. It fits the space and gets the job done efficiently.
