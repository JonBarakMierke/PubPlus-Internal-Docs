# Signs That Last

Maaco Montgomery

![Man in bucket truck installing an outdoor sign](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/195/01KTAB07F2W9QXAWPYRVGGMZY3.jpg)

### Durable Materials

For this Maaco location, we used MaxMetal® with a UV laminate finish, a combination that offers strength, weather resistance, and a clean professional look. By printing directly onto the material, we eliminate the need for vinyl overlays, which can peel or fade over time. The result is a smooth, vibrant finish that’s built to last.

### Custom Shape, Precision Cut

This project also highlights our cut-to-shape capabilities. We can produce signage that goes beyond standard rectangles, giving businesses the freedom to create shapes that fit their brand and building perfectly. From simple contour cuts to complex outlines, we tailor each piece to the project.

![Man in bucket truck installing an outdoor sign](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/196/01KTAB20FYSNCCN833TKDJH9FW.jpg)

![A man in a bucket truck installing an outdoor sign](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/197/01KTAB3TY5DNYC7BSYXGHMR3S1.jpg)

### A Fresh Look That Stands Out

The finished sign gives the Maaco building a refreshed, professional appearance that better represents their brand & service quality. With durable materials, precise cutting, and direct printing, the new signage will look great for years to come.
