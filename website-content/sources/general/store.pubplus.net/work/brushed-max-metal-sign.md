# Brushed Max Metal® Sign

YMCA of Greater Montgomery

![Brushed silver sign inside building with wooden structure](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/258/01KTACPWXVB4DWK0C7NFZ6XZQN.webp)

### Fits the space

This project at YMCA Camp Chandler was a definite highlight of 2024. 
This sign just belongs in this space. The brushed silver Max Metal® panel sits beautifully against the warm wood tones of the cabin interior. It’s simple, modern, and clean, but somehow feels classic too. 
 
The contrast between sleek metal and rustic wood creates a balance that draws your eye without disrupting the space. It complements the architecture rather than competing with it, making it feel like it’s always been there.

### Hidden Snap Standoffs

While the sign itself makes a statement, part of its sleek appeal comes from what you don’t see: the mounting hardware. Hidden snap standoffs provide a clean, floating look by keeping all fasteners tucked out of sight. 
 
Unlike traditional standoffs that show through the corners, these create a more refined, gallery-style presentation. It’s a subtle touch, but one that adds a polished, professional finish, giving the illusion that the sign is hovering just off the wall.

![Brushed silver MaxMetal sign on wooden background](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/259/01KTACR5X0W4F72SN6XE5MX38C.webp)

![Man on ladder installing silver indoor sign on wooden background](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/260/01KTACSCCQ6T3T7VA7606J22PH.webp)

### A Collaborative Effort

Signs like this don’t happen in a vacuum; they come to life through collaboration. From the first on-site meeting, our team worked hand-in-hand with the YMCA team to consider placement, materials, and design. It’s in these face-to-face conversations that real creativity kicks in. 
When a client says, “We want it to pop,” we dig into our toolbox of materials, finishes, and mounting options to make that vision a reality. That back-and-forth is where our “You think it, we print it” promise really shines, resulting in a sign that not only works for the space but reflects the spirit of the organization itself. 
 
**Update:** We’re proud to share that this project earned a 2025 Award of Excellence from PIAS (Printing Industry Association of the South)!
