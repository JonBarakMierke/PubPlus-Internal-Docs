# Discovering the Emerald Coast

Okaloosa County Board

[Learn What We Can Do](https://pubplus.net/product-listing-category/magazines-product/)

![A detailed view of the front of the magazine and some inner pages.](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/177/01KT79KD20GQ604EP6ZHSCB16Y.jpg)

### A guide to adventure

Destin–Fort Walton Beach is known for its emerald-green waters, sugar-white sand, and endless opportunities for adventure. The Discover: Adventure in Destin–Fort Walton Beach magazine was created to help visitors and locals explore everything the area has to offer. From travel tips and local highlights to recipes and family-friendly activities, the magazine serves as both an inspiration piece and a practical guide for anyone spending time along Florida’s beautiful Emerald Coast. At PubPlus, we love helping our customers share their story in a way that informs, inspires, and engages their audience, and this publication does exactly that.

### Designed to feel as good as it looks

Print is powerful when it’s done right, and this magazine is a great example of how thoughtful production choices elevate the entire experience. The soft-touch aqueous cover finish gives the magazine a smooth, velvety feel that immediately signals quality the moment someone picks it up. Paired with perfect binding, the finished piece has the look and feel of a premium publication, durable, polished, and built to be kept rather than tossed aside. Details like these make a big difference.

![A close look at the magazine's soft touch finish on the cover.](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/178/01KT79KR36TG13D3NESHPMHKZK.jpg)

![The back of the soft touch cover on the magazine. Featuring a kid fishing with a net.](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/179/01KT79M60KVND61825EFCC5J1D.jpg)

### Something for everyone

One of the things we love about this magazine is how it invites readers of all ages to engage with it. Alongside travel ideas and local recommendations, the magazine also includes recipes inspired by the region and fun activities and games for kids, making it a great companion for families visiting the area. Projects like this highlight the power of print to bring information, creativity, and entertainment together in one place. We’re proud to help organizations like the Okaloosa County Board create publications that connect with their audience and showcase what makes their community special.
