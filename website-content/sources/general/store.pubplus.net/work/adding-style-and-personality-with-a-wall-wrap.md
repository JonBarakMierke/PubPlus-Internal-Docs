# Adding Style and Personality with a Wall Wrap

YMCA of Greater Montgomery

![A modern gym with a gray & yellow full cover wall wrap](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/220/01KTABBN30E2YTSXDJWNCHWWEH.webp)

### Looks Permanent, Comes Off Easy

Sometimes you want to refresh a space, but a permanent change feels like too much commitment. Maybe you’ll use the room differently in the future, or your branding might evolve. That’s where wall wraps shine. They’re designed to look like they’ve always been part of the room, but when it’s time for a change, they peel away cleanly and easily.

### Going for the Textured Look

Wall wraps are usually applied to smooth, flat walls, but for the YMCA of Greater Montgomery’s new techno gym at their Bell Road branch, we had a unique challenge: cinderblock. To make it work, we used a heat gun to carefully contour the material to the wall’s texture. The result? A wrap that not only adhered securely but looked like it was painted directly onto the surface. It blended in so naturally that you’d swear it had been there all along.

![Close look at wall wrap contoured to brick wall](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/221/01KTABD9JN56Z52NNWAF8GKRYK.webp)

![Gay & yellow full wall wrap in modern gym](https://pubplus-portal-v1.s3.us-east-2.amazonaws.com/primary/222/01KTABER4M8YSYRC13ZA6RVS1R.webp)

### One Material, Endless Possibilities

Over the past couple of years, wall wraps have become one of our favorite signage solutions, and it’s easy to see why. They’re incredibly versatile, transforming spaces in gyms, conference rooms, restaurants, offices, and beyond. If you can dream it, there’s a wall wrap that can bring it to life.
