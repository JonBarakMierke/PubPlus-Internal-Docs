Publications Plus has been serving businesses, nonprofits, and creatives from Montgomery, Alabama since 1986. Whether you need a short run of business cards, a full-color magazine, large-format event signage, or a targeted direct-mail campaign, our in-house press team handles every stage of production under one roof — from pre-press and color management through binding, finishing, and shipping. You can place orders any time at [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net) without creating an account, and payments are processed securely through Stripe.

## Our Press Departments

Every order flows through the department best suited to it. Here's a quick look at what happens behind the scenes:

| Department | Specialization |
| --- | --- |
| **Pre-Press** | File review, color proofing, and plate-making |
| **Heat-Set** | Long-run coated and uncoated roll printing |
| **Sheet-Fed** | Thicker paper stocks, UV coating, high-end finishing |
| **Wide-Format** | Large banners, posters, and display graphics |
| **Digital** | Short-run, fast-turnaround color and B&W printing |
| **Direct Mailing** | EDDM® campaign preparation and postal coordination |
| **Binding & Finishing** | Saddle-stitch, perfect-bind, lamination, die-cut |
| **Design** | In-house design services if you need artwork created |
