The Publications Plus client portal gives you a centralized place to manage every aspect of your relationship with us — from reviewing past orders and tracking active print jobs to updating your contact and billing information. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning customer, signing in takes just a few seconds at [client.pubplus.net](https://client.pubplus.net/).

## Logging In

Navigate to [client.pubplus.net](https://client.pubplus.net/) and enter the email address and password associated with your Publications Plus account. Once your credentials are accepted, you'll land on your account dashboard.

### Remember Me

Check the **Remember Me** box on the login screen if you want your browser to keep you signed in between sessions. This is convenient on a personal computer but should be left unchecked on any shared or public device.

<Warning>
  Never enable **Remember Me** on a shared, public, or workplace computer that other people can access. Doing so may allow others to view your order history and account details.
</Warning>

## Resetting a Forgotten Password

If you can't remember your password, you can request a reset link at any time — no need to contact support first.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Forgot Password page">
    On the login screen at [client.pubplus.net](https://client.pubplus.net/), click the **Forgot Password?** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address associated with your account into the field provided and click **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    Open your email client and look for a message from Publications Plus with the subject line containing a password reset link. Check your spam or junk folder if it doesn't appear within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the reset link">
    Click the link inside the email. It will open a secure page in your browser. Note that reset links expire after a short period — if yours has expired, simply repeat the process to request a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new password">
    Enter your new password, confirm it in the second field, and click **Save**. You'll be redirected to the login screen where you can sign in with your updated credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Choose a strong, unique password that you don't reuse on other websites. A mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols works best.
</Tip>

## What You Can Do in the Portal

Once logged in, the client portal puts your account information and order activity at your fingertips.

<Accordion title="View and track orders">
  Review the full history of every order you've placed with Publications Plus, including itemized details, quantities, and specifications. Active orders display real-time status updates so you always know where your print job stands.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Manage account details">
  Update your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address at any time from the account settings page. Keeping your contact details current ensures you receive order confirmations and shipping notifications without delay.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Access invoices and receipts">
  Download or print invoices for any completed order directly from the portal. This is especially useful for expense reporting or record-keeping purposes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Business account management">
  If you have a business account with Publications Plus, the portal lets you manage billing preferences and keep your business information up to date. Reach out to our team to learn more about setting up a business account.
</Accordion>
