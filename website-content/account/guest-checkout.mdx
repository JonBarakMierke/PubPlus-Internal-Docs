You don't need to create an account to order from Publications Plus. Guest checkout lets you browse the full product catalog at [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net/), configure your print job, and pay securely — all without signing up or remembering another password. Your order is confirmed by email the moment your payment clears.

## How Guest Checkout Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the store">
    Head to [store.pubplus.net](https://store.pubplus.net/) and explore the product catalog. Use the category navigation or search bar to find the print product you need — business cards, flyers, banners, booklets, and more are all available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your product and add it to your cart">
    Select your desired product, then choose your specifications: size, paper stock, quantity, turnaround time, and any finishing options. Once you're satisfied with your selections, click **Add to Cart**. You can continue shopping and add multiple items before checking out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proceed to checkout">
    When you're ready, open your cart and click **Checkout**. You'll be prompted to choose between creating an account or continuing as a guest — select **Continue as Guest** to proceed without registering.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your contact and shipping details">
    Fill in your name, email address, phone number, and the shipping address where you'd like your order delivered. Double-check these details carefully — your order confirmation and shipping updates will be sent to the email address you provide here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay securely with Stripe">
    Publications Plus processes all payments through **Stripe**, a leading payment platform trusted by millions of businesses worldwide. Enter your credit or debit card details — Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and all other major cards are accepted. Your card information is encrypted and never stored on our servers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your order confirmation">
    After your payment is approved, you'll receive an order confirmation email at the address you provided. This email includes your order number, a summary of what you ordered, and an estimated turnaround time. Keep this email handy — you'll need your order number if you ever need to contact support about your order.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Guest orders receive the same email updates as account holders. You'll get notifications when your order moves into production, when it ships, and when it's out for delivery — including a tracking number so you can follow your package every step of the way.
</Tip>

## Payment Information

Publications Plus uses [Stripe](https://stripe.com/) for all payment processing. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant, meaning your card data is handled according to the highest industry security standards.

**Accepted payment methods include:**

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover
- Other major credit and debit cards

<Note>
  At this time, guest checkout supports card payments only. If you need to pay by invoice, ACH transfer, or another method, a business account is required. Contact us at [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) or **(877) 701-5087** to discuss your options.
</Note>

## Repeat Customer? Consider a Business Account

If you place orders with Publications Plus on a regular basis, a business account may be a better fit for you. Business accounts unlock additional billing options, faster reordering from your order history, and dedicated support from our team.

<Accordion title="Benefits of a business account">
  - Access your full order history at any time through the client portal
  - Reorder previous jobs in just a few clicks
  - Additional payment and billing flexibility, including invoicing options
  - Dedicated account support from the Publications Plus team
</Accordion>

To set up a business account, reach out to our support team at [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net) or call **(877) 701-5087**. We'll get you set up quickly so your next order is even easier.
