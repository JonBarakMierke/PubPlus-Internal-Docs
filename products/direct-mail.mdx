## EDDM® — Every Door Direct Mail

### What Is EDDM®?

Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM®) is a USPS program that lets you saturate an entire mail carrier route — every residential or business address on a route — without purchasing or maintaining a mailing list. You select the postal routes that cover your target neighborhoods, we print your pieces to USPS-approved specifications, and USPS delivers one to every door on those routes.

**EDDM® is ideal when you want to:**

- Reach every household in a neighborhood, ZIP code, or radius around your location
- Promote a grand opening, seasonal sale, or local event
- Build brand awareness in a defined geographic area
- Skip the cost and complexity of a targeted mailing list

### How EDDM® Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Routes">
    Use the USPS EDDM® online mapping tool to select carrier routes by ZIP code, city, or radius. Filter by demographics like household size or average income. Share your selected routes with us when you request a quote.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design Your Mailer">
    EDDM® pieces must meet USPS size requirements (minimum 6.125&quot; × 11&quot; for flat-size EDDM®). Our team can verify your dimensions and bleed setup before you finalize your design.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We Print & Prep">
    We print your mailers, bundle them per USPS regulations, attach the required EDDM® indicia, and prepare them for drop-off at the appropriate postal facility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="USPS Delivers">
    USPS delivers your piece to every address on your selected routes — no stamps, no address list, no individual addressing required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  EDDM® postage rates are among the lowest available through USPS, making it a cost-effective choice for high-volume, geographically targeted campaigns. Ask us to include postage costs in your quote.
</Note>
