<Note>

</Note>

## Sign Hardware

Hardware is sold separately. Choose the mounting style that works best for your property.

### Metal H-Stake

A standard wire h-stake that pushes directly into the ground — the fastest way to post a yard sign.

- **Price:** $1.80 each
- **Quantity:** 1–100 per order

### Wooden Stake

White wooden stakes available in four lengths. Pair a 4 ft stake with subdivision real estate signs for the best visibility.

- **Price:** $5.94 each
- **Lengths:** 2 ft, 3 ft, 4 ft, 5 ft
- **Color:** White
- **Quantity:** 1–100 per order

<Note>
  Wooden stakes are sold without mounting hardware. Add a plastic hardware set to your order to attach the sign panel to the stake.
</Note>

### Plastic Hardware Set

The small plastic clips that lock a sign panel onto a wooden stake. A typical real estate sign requires **2 sets**.

- **Price:** $0.80 per set
- **Quantity:** 1–100 sets per order

## How to Order

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the online store">
    Go to [store.pubplus.net/store](https://store.pubplus.net/store) and find **Subdivision Real Estate Signs** under the Signage category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your subdivision">
    Select your community from the dropdown. The design is automatically applied — no artwork upload needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your realtor details">
    Type in your **Realtor Name** and **Realtor Phone** (both required). Optionally add a brokerage name or any special notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your quantity">
    Choose 1–5 signs. Each sign is $72.00.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add hardware to your cart">
    Navigate back to the store and add your preferred hardware — a wooden stake, metal h-stake, or plastic hardware sets — as separate line items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out">
    Complete your order online. No account required for guest checkout. You'll receive an order confirmation by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>
