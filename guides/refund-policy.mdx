Because we specialize in custom-printed products made to order, our refund policy is tailored to reflect the nature of that work. Once production begins on a custom job, materials and labor are committed to your specific order — so refunds are limited and depend on the circumstances of the issue. Review the sections below carefully before placing your order, and reach out before initiating any dispute so we can make it right as quickly as possible.

<Accordion title="1. Custom Orders">
  Custom orders cannot be refunded once production has begun. If you need to cancel, contact support before your order enters production to discuss your options. Once a job is in production, a refund is only available if there is a verified defect or production error on the part of Publications Plus.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. Proof Approval">
  You are responsible for reviewing and approving all artwork and proofs before your order goes to press. Once you approve a design, it is considered final. Publications Plus is not liable for errors — including typos, color variations, or layout issues — that were present in an approved proof. Review every detail carefully before giving your approval.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Errors & Defects">
  If your product arrives damaged or is printed incorrectly due to an error on our part, we will reprint or replace the affected items at no cost to you. Contact us promptly with photos or documentation of the issue so we can process your replacement as quickly as possible.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. Chargebacks">
  If you experience a problem with your order, contact Publications Plus directly before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Most issues can be resolved quickly through direct communication, and a chargeback can delay that resolution significantly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Non-Refundable Items">
  Shipping costs and completed custom production work are non-refundable unless otherwise required by applicable law. This applies regardless of whether the order has been delivered, as the work and materials have already been consumed in fulfilling your job.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  If you have a concern about your order, reach out to us before initiating a chargeback or dispute — most issues can be resolved quickly and directly.

  - **Email:** support@pubplus.net
  - **Phone:** (877) 701-5087
</Note>

<Warning>
  Approving a proof is final. Double-check all text, spelling, layout, and colors before submitting your approval — Publications Plus cannot be held responsible for errors present in customer-approved artwork.
</Warning>
