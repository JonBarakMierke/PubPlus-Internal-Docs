Publications Plus, Inc. is committed to handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected when you use the website or place an order, how that data is used, and what rights you have regarding your information. By using the Publications Plus website or purchasing products, you agree to the practices described below. If you have questions or requests related to your personal data, contact support@pubplus.net.

<Accordion title="1. Information We Collect">
  When you place an order or interact with the Publications Plus website, the following information may be collected:

  - **Personal details:** Name, email address, billing information, and shipping address
  - **Order history:** Details of past and current orders
  - **Technical data:** IP address and browser information collected automatically when you visit the site

  This information is used to fulfill your orders, communicate with you, and improve the overall experience on the platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. How We Use Your Information">
  Publications Plus uses the information collected to:

  - Process and fulfill your orders
  - Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  - Improve products, services, and the website experience
  - Detect and prevent fraud and unauthorized activity

  Your information is not sold to third parties for marketing purposes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Third-Party Services">
  Publications Plus works with select third-party service providers to operate the business:

  - **Stripe** is used for secure payment processing. When you make a purchase, your payment information is handled directly by Stripe and subject to their privacy policy.
  - **Analytics tools** may be used to understand site usage and improve performance.

  Each of these third-party services operates under its own privacy policy. Publications Plus encourages you to review those policies independently.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. QR Code & Tracking Data">
  <Info>
    Some products ordered through Publications Plus may include QR codes. When those QR codes are scanned, tracking data is collected for reporting and analytics purposes. See below for details on what is tracked.
  </Info>

  Some products may include QR codes that track scan activity. When a QR code on your product is scanned by an end user, the following data may be recorded:

  - **Time** of the scan
  - **Device type** used to scan
  - **Approximate location** of the scan

  This data is used for reporting and analytics purposes — for example, to help you understand how your printed materials are being engaged with. If your product includes a QR code, be aware that scan-level data is being collected and consider disclosing this to your own customers or recipients as appropriate.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Data Retention">
  Order and customer data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill business operations and comply with legal obligations. This includes keeping records of transactions, communications, and order history for accounting, support, and compliance purposes. When data is no longer required, it is securely disposed of.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="6. Your Rights">
  You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data that Publications Plus holds about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact the support team directly:

  - **Email:** support@pubplus.net
  - **Phone:** (877) 701-5087

  Publications Plus will respond to your request in a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law.
</Accordion>
