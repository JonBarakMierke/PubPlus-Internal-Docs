From the moment your job is approved and sent to press, Publications Plus works to produce and deliver your order as efficiently as possible. This guide covers everything you need to know about how long production takes, how your order ships, how to track it, and how to pick it up locally if you're in the Montgomery, AL area.

## Production Times

Production time begins after your files are approved and your order is confirmed. The timeline varies based on the type of printing your job requires:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Digital Printing" icon="print">
    **2–3 business days** for most digital print jobs. Ideal for shorter runs and faster turnaround.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Offset & Wide-Format" icon="expand">
    **5–7 business days** for offset-printed and wide-format jobs. These processes require additional setup and drying time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simple Jobs" icon="bolt">
    **Under 1 hour** for straightforward, low-complexity jobs that are already print-ready. Ask about same-day availability.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Complex or large-volume jobs — such as multi-piece direct mail campaigns, large-format installations, or specialty finishing — may require **several additional business days** beyond the standard timeline. We'll give you an accurate production estimate when you place your order or request a quote.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Share your deadline when placing your order. If you're working against a hard date, let us know upfront so we can assess whether a rush option is available for your job.
</Tip>

## Shipping Carriers

Publications Plus works with a network of trusted carrier partners to deliver your order safely, whether it's a small package or a full freight shipment.

| Carrier | Best For |
|---------|----------|
| **UPS® Ground** | Standard parcel delivery for most print orders |
| **T-Force (UPS® Freight)** | Freight-level shipments via the UPS network |
| **FedEx® Freight** | Freight delivery for larger or heavier orders |
| **RNL Carriers** | Regional freight and specialty delivery |
| **Southeast Freight** | Regional LTL freight across the Southeast |
| **AAA Cooper** | Regional LTL freight with strong Southeast coverage |
| **XPO** | National LTL freight for larger shipments |
| **MACS Courier** | Local courier delivery for time-sensitive orders |

We select the most appropriate carrier for your shipment based on size, weight, destination, and delivery requirements. Shipping methods include:

- **Local delivery** — for customers in the Montgomery, AL area
- **Courier services** — for time-sensitive or same-day local deliveries
- **Commercial truck lines** — for freight-level print orders

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an **email with tracking information** so you can monitor your delivery. Keep an eye on your inbox (and your spam folder) after your estimated production window closes.

If you haven't received tracking information within the expected timeframe, contact our support team:

- **Email:** [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net)
- **Phone:** (877) 701-5087

<Warning>
  Publications Plus is not responsible for delays caused by carriers, weather events, or other external factors outside our control. Carrier-provided delivery estimates are not guarantees. If your shipment is delayed, contact the carrier directly using your tracking number, or reach out to our team for assistance.
</Warning>

## Lost or Damaged Orders

Despite careful packaging and trusted carrier partners, occasional issues can happen in transit. If your order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive at all, here's what to do:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Document the Damage">
    If your shipment arrives damaged, take clear photos of the outer packaging and the damaged contents before discarding anything. This documentation is required for a carrier claim.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Publications Plus Support">
    Reach out to our team as soon as possible:

    - **Email:** [support@pubplus.net](mailto:support@pubplus.net)
    - **Phone:** (877) 701-5087

    Provide your order number, a description of the issue, and any photos of damaged items.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We'll Assist With Replacement or Claim">
    Our support team will work with you to resolve the issue — whether that means filing a claim with the carrier or coordinating a replacement order. We're here to make sure you get what you paid for.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Please report damaged or lost orders as promptly as possible. Carrier claim windows are time-sensitive, and delays in reporting may limit the options available for resolution.
</Note>
